We headed out another side door and found ourselves in a staff corridor. An elevator ahead had a broom blocking the door from closing. We piled in, Rooster kicked the broom away.

When the door opened, Dolph stood there. He directed us out a door and directly into a waiting Econoline van. Two staff bartenders and bar servers waited for us.
