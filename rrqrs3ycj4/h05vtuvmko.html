A couple hours into my dealing shift at the restricted table, Snake approached my table. I had just raked the pot to the winner after the showdown when I finally turned to look at him.

In one fluid move, he raised two fingers of his left hand to mustache as if casually brushing it, then a thumb toward the bar, while he raised his right hand palm up.

An alert for trouble on the bar side, no need start imposing betting limits. So it was not cops or the state.

I just kept dealing.

About a poker hand later, three of Dolph's guys appeared in the card room. Goose and Yogi took up positions at the side door. Lugnut at the card room to bar corridor.
